ABOUT HIMS

HIMS is a state of the art institute situated in a well-designed spacious structure, right in the middle of 3 famous educational institutions, in an easily accessible locality of Gulabad, Phase III Chowk, Jamrud Road, Peshawar. UET comprises on more than 10 Lecture Halls, a Science Lab, Anatomy Lab, Computer Lab, Library, Examination Hall and a Skill Lab with well-equipped and fully furnished facilities in order to implement and integrate the research, theory and practice of students.

HIMS will be one of the leading institutes in the field of Nursing & Health Sciences and will produce quality health care professionals and intellectuals to meet the need or demand of the health care industry. We have been recognized and accredited by Pakistan Nursing Council (PNC) and Higher Education Regulatory Authority (HERA).

ACADEMICS

BACHELORS IN NURSING (BSN) 4 YEARS

The BSN program is spread over 8 semesters and the total credit hours for the overall program is 139, designed by the Higher Education Commission (HEC). The program is recognized by the Pakistan Nursing Council (PNC) and affiliated with Khyber Medical University (KMU), Peshawar.

Learn More

POST REGISTERED NURSE (POST RN BSN) 2 YEARS

The Post RN BSN program is spread over 4 semesters and the total credit hours of curriculum designed and recognized by Pakistan Nursing Council (PNC) for the overall program is 60. The program is affiliated with Khyber Medical University (KMU) Peshawar.
